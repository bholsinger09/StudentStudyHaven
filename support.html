‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen, enter your email and password, and you're ready to go! You can also sign in with your existing account credentials.

How do I add a new class?

Navigate to the Classes tab, tap the "+" button, and enter your course details including name, instructor, schedule, and location.

Can I organize my notes by class?

Yes! When creating a note, you can assign it to a specific class. You can also link related notes together for better organization.

How do flashcards work?

Create flashcard decks by tapping the "+" button in the Flashcards tab. Add front (question) and back (answer) content, then practice by flipping through your cards.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use Firebase Authentication and secure cloud storage to protect your data. All information is encrypted and stored safely.

Can I access my data on multiple devices?

Yes! Your data syncs across all devices where you're signed in with the same account.

What if I forgot my password?

Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

How do I delete my account?

To permanently delete your account:

  1. Open the Student Study Haven app
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab
  3. Scroll down to the "Danger Zone" section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Enter your email address to confirm
  6. Tap "Delete Account" to permanently delete

If you need assistance with account deletion, please contact us at support@studentstudyhaven.com

üîß Troubleshooting

App is crashing or freezing

  • Force quit the app and restart it
  • Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your iPhone
  • Check for iOS updates
  • If the problem persists, contact support

Data not syncing

  • Check your internet connection
  • Sign out and sign back in
  • Ensure you're using the same account on all devices
  • Wait a few moments for sync to complete

Can't log in

  • Double-check your email and password
  • Use "Forgot Password" to reset if needed
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Try closing and reopening the app

Features not working correctly

  • Update to the latest app version
  • Clear the app cache by signing out and back in
  • Contact support with specific details about the issue
