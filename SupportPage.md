## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. I can't log in to my account.
- Ensure you are using the correct email and password.
- If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen.
- If you still have trouble, contact support.

### 2. My data is not syncing across devices.
- Make sure you are connected to the internet.
- Try logging out and back in.
- If the issue persists, contact support.

### 3. The app is crashing or freezing.
- Restart your device and try again.
- Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed.
- If problems continue, contact support with details about your device and iOS version.

### 4. How do I delete my account?
- Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account.
- Confirm your decision. All data will be permanently removed.

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Restart the app and your device.
2. Check for app updates in the App Store.
3. Ensure your device is running the latest iOS version.
4. Contact support if issues persist.
